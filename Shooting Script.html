Segment 2 · 零售現場 — 遠東百貨 · 17 歲

約 55 秒 · 原 95 秒壓縮
Voiceover · cut down

"2023. I was 17. Floor supervisor intern at Far Eastern Department Stores — youngest in the company's history. Two floors, forty brands, menswear and sportswear. Most of the counter staff had been in retail longer than I'd been alive."

— 說快一點。這段的質感在數字,不在故事。

"Every morning, thirty minutes before the manager's floor walk: pull reports, build the analysis — DoD, MoM, YoY — and have a reason for every number that moved. Briefs, planograms, POPs, complaints, inventory. I built a shared dashboard in three days because nobody could track me across both floors."

"Then — Mother's Day. Highest-spending window of the year. Dominant shopper: women and families. My floors: men and sport. A mismatch."

"We pitched the manager a free in-store yoga series, three times a week, using the cinema's footfall as the hook. Not a discount. A reason to walk a floor they'd skip."

"Visits up nearly 20%. Mother's Day revenue +15% YoY. Conversion +8 points. The category question isn't always what's on the shelf — sometimes it's who isn't walking past it yet."

Look
A → B 熱桌中景開場,「Mother's Day」這句切到玻璃會議室特寫。
Shots
A · 中景 35mm
B · 中近景 50mm
數字字卡
指尖滑螢幕
Movement
緩推 5% 「Mother's Day」 那拍用穩定器走進特寫,鎖住。其餘靜態。
Delivery
數字講平,不要錄調。讓資料聽起來像事實,不是業績。每個百分比後停滿一拍。
B-roll
無聲插入:筆電上一個乾淨的表格畫面(自製 mock,不可出現真實公司資料)。熱桌上俯拍。
Cut beats
CUT 「A mismatch.」 硬切到玻璃室特寫。光變了,能量也變了。

Segment 3 · 資料思考 — Outlier / Scale AI

約 55 秒 · 原 75 秒壓縮
Voiceover · cut down

"Outlier has nothing to do with FMCG. I want to be upfront about that."

"It's a Scale AI subsidiary. I evaluate AI model outputs — multimodal data, rubric design, quality across thirty-plus projects, three or four running in parallel."

"You start as a contributor. Hit consistent quality and you're promoted to Super Attempter — now you're also helping other contributors hit the bar. That's where it got interesting."

"Part of my role: co-host contributor webinars. The Quality Manager knows what good looks like. My job was to translate that into something a contributor could act on tomorrow."

"Take something dense and ambiguous, build a structure around it, communicate it so it drives consistent action. That's not an AI skill. It's a thinking pattern."

"In S&CM — sitting across from a buyer, arguing why a planogram should change, why a category deserves more shelf — you're doing the same thing. Translating data into something another person can act on."

Look
B · 玻璃室特寫 整段不換景。更緊、更分析性的能量。
Lens
50mm · f/2.0 · 特寫,眼睛在上三分之一,背景柔散。
Movement
完全鎖定 只用三腳架。內容是結構性的,鏡頭也要是。
Delivery
比 S2 更慢。這是「我怎麼想」的段。「That's not an AI skill. It's a thinking pattern.」前後各停滿半秒。
B-roll
可選:筆電螢幕上的匿名 rubric / outline 文件 — 越肩鏡頭,35mm,2 秒。不要出現任何真實 Outlier 內容。
Cut beats
CUT 4:40 版已刪去 「Just in a very different room」。改在 「act on」 收尾,硬切到 S4 走拍鏡頭。

Segment 4 · 跨文化視角 — 台北 ↔ 巴黎

約 55 秒 · 原 80 秒壓縮
Voiceover · cut down

"I transferred to ESCP Paris by choice. Not because I wanted to live abroad — because I wanted to be in a market I didn't already understand."

"In Taiwan, a convenience store is infrastructure. Food, bills, parcels, printing, coffee, 2am. Everything in one place."

"In France, that doesn't exist. Supermarket for food, pharmacy for skincare, tabac for the rest. Different pace, distributed system."

"And the pharmacy is the part I find most relevant for NIVEA."

"In Taiwan, skincare lives next to promotions, KOL claims, whitening labels. The shelf shouts what it does. In France, the pharmacy reads closer to dermatology than beauty — the brands that win win on trust."

"My French is still A2. But when you're not fluent, you notice differently — where signage breaks, where a foreign shopper gets lost. That's a perspective a native doesn't get for free."

Look
走拍 → A 走廊走拍開場,結尾回到熱桌中景講最後一句。
Lens
35mm · f/2.8 上穩定器 · 主角領先攝影機約 1 米,操作員倒退走,需有人在後方護航。
Movement
走 慢、不慌。「the pharmacy is the part…」 切到 鎖定中景。
B-roll
兩個空鏡是這段的關鍵:
① 台灣便利商店內部 — 如果你手機裡有舊素材就用,沒有就刪掉,不要刻意擺拍。
② 巴黎藥局外的綠十字 — 進出 WeWork 順手拍。Rue de Madrid 200 米內至少有兩家。
Delivery
好奇的語氣,不是學術的。走路讓「比較」這件事有身體感。
Cut beats
CUT 「pharmacy」 這個字落地前,綠十字空鏡多停 1.5 秒。

Segment 5 · 為什麼是這個品牌

約 60 秒 · 原 90 秒壓縮
逐字稿 · 4:40 壓縮版

"Why this brand. Not because it's big — because it has a specific, interesting problem."

"Beiersdorf's group organic sales grew 2.4% last year. NIVEA grew 0.9%. Volume effect was negative — meaning the growth came from price, not from more people buying more."

— 數字講平,跟 S2 一樣。

"For a brand at ~5.5 billion euros that's not a crisis. It's a signal. Skincare has fragmented — derma, ingredient, K-beauty, clean beauty — and every one of them talks to consumers more specifically than a mass brand can."

"So the real question: how does a mass brand stay relevant when the market keeps moving toward niche?"

"The answer they're testing — the campaign on loneliness and connection — sounds unrelated to moisturiser. But it makes sense: the brand isn't built on skin care, it's built on care. Moving care from product to human is consistent, not a pivot."

"And the same blue tin sits in Watsons in Taipei and in a French pharmacy — completely different trust logic. That's the gap I want to spend time on. Not because I have the answer. Because it's the right question."

Look
A · 中景 回到熱桌。回家講主論。跟 S1 完全相同構圖 — 視覺押韻。
Lens
35mm · f/2.8 · 故意跟 S1 一模一樣。
Movement
靜態 + 「the right question」 最後 6 秒 緩推 3%。
Delivery
整片最分析性的一段。數字平。「why me」的能量絕對不能滲進來 — 這段是在講品牌的問題,不是你的適配度。
On-screen text
可加下三分之一字卡 「+2.4%」、「+0.9%」、「~€5.5bn」— 小等寬字、左下角、各 1.2 秒。v1 版可選。
Cut beats
CUT 「the right question」 這句要切到全片最緊的構圖。這是整支片的修辭樞紐。

§ B

現場流程 · 拍攝順序

約 3.5 小時

拍攝順序

  1. 09:30 到 WeWork。確認預訂空間(熱桌 + 會議室)。看窗戶日照方向。
  2. 09:45 換裝 + 領夾麥測試。錄 30 秒環境音。
  3. 10:00 架設 機位 A(熱桌)。構圖、對焦、音量。
  4. 10:15 連拍 S1 / S5 / S6 — 同一個設置,各三 take。不要中途換景。
  5. 11:00 移到 機位 B(玻璃室)。重打光。
  6. 11:15 拍 S3 + S2 母親節特寫補拍。
  7. 11:45 回 機位 A 拍 S2 寬景。
  8. 12:15 走廊拍 S4 走拍。至少 3 趟。
  9. 12:30 空鏡衝刺:Rue de Madrid 藥局綠十字、街景、大樓入口。
  10. 12:45 收工。離開前先備份記憶卡。

表演筆記

  • 能量曲線: S1 低 → S2 累積 → S3 靜止+分析 → S4 好奇 → S5 最嚴謹 → S6 沉穩。不要太早衝到頂。
  • 數字是事實,不是業績。 每個百分比都唸平。觀眾自己會決定厲不厲害。
  • 每個關鍵主張後停半秒。信任沉默,不要填滿它。
  • 視線 敘事段略偏鏡頭,只有三句主論句直視鏡頭:S1 引言、S5 提問、S6 引言。
  • 手 中景時要入鏡。講數字時不要手勢,只有講推論的時候才用手。
  • 如果一個 take 卡詞,從上一個完整句子重來,不要從上個字接。剪接點比較乾淨。

燈光 · 用 WeWork 的窗光當主光

  • 主光: 北向窗 45° 在攝影機右側。太陽穿透時,加 ND 或薄紗。
  • 補光: 銀色反光板在攝影機左,離主角約 1.2 米。光比目標 ~3:1。
  • 背景: 讓 WeWork 內部柔散成 bokeh — 畫面內不要出現品牌 logo,不要拍到其他人。
  • 玻璃室: 關走廊側百葉,控制主角後方玻璃反光。
  • 白平衡: 5200K 鎖死。不要自動。
  • 時段: 中午前拍完 — 巴黎五月的光在 10:00–12:00 之間最柔。

音訊 · 自拍片最常死在這裡

  • 領夾位置: 下巴下方 15 公分,藏在領口下。針織布記得測磨擦聲。
  • 備份錄音 用手機 Voice Memo,放桌上鏡頭外。每個 take 開拍前拍手對時。
  • 關空調 如果 WeWork 允許。不行的話,離出風口 1 米以上。
  • 茶水間咖啡機 會毀掉一整個 take。每次開錄前先聽。
  • 每個機位 30 秒環境音 — 開拍前先錄,不要後錄。
  • 音量: 峰值 ‑12dB,底噪 ‑40dB。全程戴耳機。

§ C

後製 · 剪輯與交付

CapCut / Premiere · 24fps · H.264

剪輯順序

  1. 組合剪:所有 take 接成一條,先別修。看一遍。
  2. 每段選一個主 take — 看表達,不看臉。
  3. 收緊到 5:00 的粗剪。先不要擔心超時。
  4. 鎖死 S1 → S6 主軸,然後狠剪到畫面定剪 4:25。
  5. 空鏡只放有功能的:藥局綠十字、表格、走拍。不要塞填料。
  6. S2 + S5 加下三分之一數字字卡(v2 可選)。
  7. 混音:-16 LUFS 整體。人聲輕壓縮。無配樂。
  8. 調色:對比 +5,色溫 +200K 偏暖,配 WeWork 室內感。
  9. 片尾卡 15 秒。總長 4:40。輸出 3840×2160 · 24fps · H.264。
