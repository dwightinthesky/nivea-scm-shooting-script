運鏡哲學 · 為什麼兩支 iPhone 可以做到大師級

Working principle

大師級不是「器材好」,是「決定堅定」。
每一個鏡位、每一個運鏡、每一個剪接點都有一個非它不可的理由。

整支片我們只用 三種運鏡(Locked / Slow Push / Walk),只用 三個鏡位(機位 A / 機位 B / 走廊),只用 一種光源(WeWork 北向窗光)。重複本身就是電影語言 — 你回到同一張椅子三次,觀眾的潛意識會把它讀成「論文式結構」,這比花俏的運鏡更高級。

01 · 限制 = 紀律

不要嘗試「掩飾」器材限制。iPhone 拍出來就是 iPhone 的質感。重點是用 iPhone 的優勢(極淺景深 Cinematic mode、ProRes Log、超寬動態範圍)去做專業相機都未必能做的事。

02 · 雙機位是奢侈

大部分申請影片只有一支機。你有兩支,意思是每一個 take 都同時被兩個角度記錄。這讓你能在一次表演裡剪出 jump-cut 而不破連戲,媲美雙機位紀錄片訪談。

03 · MacBook 是隱形主角

MacBook 在拍攝現場做兩件事:① 透過連續互通相機(Continuity Camera)當外接 4K 監看螢幕,讓你能看到構圖;② 開啟備忘錄全螢幕當提詞器,字級調到從鏡頭距離剛好讀得到。

Segment 2 · Retail Execution · the department store floor

1:00
A + B 換景
逐字稿

"In 2023, when I was seventeen, I interned as a floor supervisor at Far Eastern Department Stores, responsible for two floors covering menswear and sportswear, around forty brands in total."

"Most of the counter staff had been in retail longer than I had been alive, so the first few weeks were really about listening before doing anything else."

"The moment I want to share is Mother's Day. It is one of the highest-spending retail windows of the year, and the dominant shopper is women and families. But my two floors were menswear and sportswear, so there was a clear mismatch."

"We had noticed the cinema on the eighth floor brought in a lot of footfall whenever a film was doing well, so we used that as a starting point and pitched the store manager a free in-store yoga series, three times a week throughout the campaign."

"The idea was not to rely on discounts, but to give women and families a real reason to walk onto a floor they would normally skip."

"Store visits went up by nearly twenty percent. Mother's Day revenue grew fifteen percent year on year. And visitor-to-buyer conversion improved by eight points."

"What I took from that is that the most important category question is not always about the product on the shelf. Sometimes it is about who is not walking past it yet."

Segment 3 · Data Precision · turning ambiguity into action

0:55
A 機 + B 越肩
逐字稿

"My second piece of evidence comes from a job that has nothing to do with FMCG, and I want to be upfront about that."

"Outlier is a subsidiary of Scale AI. My role there is to evaluate and improve AI model outputs across multimodal data, rubric design, and quality assessment, usually with three or four projects running at the same time."

"After producing consistent quality work, I was promoted to a role where I also help other contributors reach the same bar. Part of that role is preparing and co-hosting webinars for new contributors."

"The challenge there is always the same. The Quality Manager knows exactly what good looks like, but that knowledge is dense and full of edge cases. My job sits in the middle, which is to understand what the team really needs, and translate it into something a contributor can act on the next day."

"What I am really doing is taking something complex and ambiguous, building a clear structure around it, and communicating it in a way that drives consistent action. That is not an AI skill. It is a thinking pattern."

"And I think it is exactly the same pattern you use in S&CM, when you are sitting across from a retail buyer and explaining why a planogram should change, or why a category deserves more shelf space. You are translating data and strategy into something another person can act on."

大師運鏡
整段一個鏡位 · Lex Fridman 級單機紀律。最分析性的段落,鏡頭也最克制。整段一個 take 不換景,A 機鎖死 50 多秒,中間插一個 2 秒越肩鏡頭。

關鍵停拍

1:00

逐字稿

"And finally, why NIVEA. Not because it is a large brand, but because I think it is a brand with a very specific and interesting problem."

"Beiersdorf's group organic sales grew around 2.4 percent in 2025, and NIVEA itself grew 0.9 percent. Most of that growth came from price rather than from more people buying more product."

— 數字講平。讓資料聽起來像觀察,不是業績報告。

"For a brand at around 5.5 billion euros in sales, that is not a crisis, but I think it is a signal. Skincare has fragmented into derma, ingredient, K-beauty and clean beauty brands, and every one of them is talking to consumers in a more specific way than a mass brand can."

"So the real question NIVEA is trying to answer right now is how a mass brand stays relevant when the market keeps moving towards niche."

"And in the two markets I have lived in, the same brand and the same blue tin sit in very different environments. In Taipei, it sits in a Watsons aisle next to promotions and functional claims. In Paris, it sits in a pharmacy next to clinical, derma-style brands. The reason a shopper trusts it is not the same in those two places."

"That gap, between what a brand wants to say and what a shopper actually sees in front of them, is the part of S&CM I want to spend time on. Not because I have the answer, but because I think it is the right question."

§ D

現場流程 · 拍攝順序

約 3.5 小時

拍攝順序

  1. 09:30 到 WeWork。確認預訂空間(熱桌 + 玻璃會議室)。看窗戶日照方向。
  2. 09:45 換裝 + 兩支 iPhone 都接外接電源。MacBook 開 Continuity Camera 對 A 機。錄 30 秒環境音。
  3. 10:00 架設機位 A(熱桌)。A 機(三腳架,1.4m)+ B 機(45°,2×)+ MacBook 提詞器。對焦 + 白平衡 + 音量。
  4. 10:20 連拍 S1 / S5 / S6 — 同一機位、同一光、各 3 take。S5 最長,留多一點時間。不要中途換景。
  5. 11:15 移到玻璃會議室。重新架 A 機(0.9m,2×)+ B 機(廣角空間)。
  6. 11:30 拍 S3(整段一鏡)+ S2 母親節特寫補拍。S3 是長 take,提詞要分四頁。
  7. 12:00 回機位 A 拍 S2 寬景。
  8. 12:15 走廊拍 S4 走拍。B 機手持,至少 3 趟。需要有人在後方護航。
  9. 12:30 B 機俯拍 b-roll:MacBook 螢幕上 mock 表格。
  10. 12:45 出 WeWork,Rue de Madrid 街上拍藥局綠十字 × 2 個交叉口。
  11. 13:00 收工。離開前用 AirDrop 把所有 take 傳 MacBook 備份。

iPhone 拍攝設定 · 必做清單

  • □ Blackmagic Camera(免費)取代內建相機:能鎖白平衡、ISO、快門。
  • □ 影片格式:ProRes 4K 24fps Log(iPhone 15 Pro 以上)。
  • □ 兩支 iPhone 設定完全相同:同 ISO、同快門 1/48、同白平衡 5200K。
  • □ 對焦鎖定主角眼睛(長按螢幕直到出現 AE/AF LOCK)。
  • □ 飛航模式 ON(避免來電/通知中斷錄製)。
  • □ 兩支同時開錄 + 拍手對時(後製用)。
  • □ 每段拍完 3 take 就 AirDrop 到 MacBook 備份,避免空間爆滿。
  • □ MacBook 螢幕亮度調最低,避免反光進 iPhone 鏡頭。

燈光 · 用 WeWork 的窗光當主光

  • 主光: 北向窗 45° 在攝影機右側。陽光太強時用百葉柔化或退到牆邊。
  • 補光: 用 MacBook 開純白螢幕當銀板補光,放主角左前 1.2m。意外有效。
  • 背景: 讓 WeWork 內部柔散成 bokeh — 畫面內不要出現品牌 logo,不要拍到其他人。
  • 玻璃室: 關走廊側百葉,控制主角後方玻璃反光。
  • 白平衡: 5200K 鎖死。絕不要用自動。
  • 時段: 中午前拍完 — 巴黎五月的光在 10:00–12:00 之間最柔。

音訊 · 自拍片最常死在這裡

  • 主聲源: iPhone #2 用 Voice Memo 放在主角面前桌上(鏡頭外)。比相機麥近 3 倍。
  • 備份: A 機本身的麥克風。對時用第一聲拍手。
  • 關空調 如果 WeWork 允許。不行的話,離出風口 1 米以上。
  • 茶水間咖啡機 會毀掉一整個 take。每次開錄前先聽。
  • 每個機位 30 秒環境音 — 開拍前先錄,不要後錄。
  • 音量峰值: ‑12dB,底噪 ‑40dB。全程戴耳機。
  • 後製: Final Cut 內建 Voice Isolation 效果可以救 90% 的問題。
§ E

後製 · MacBook Air M4 / Final Cut Pro

24fps · H.264 · -16 LUFS

剪輯順序

  1. 所有 take AirDrop 進 MacBook,用拍手對時對齊兩支 iPhone 的 take。
  2. 組合剪:所有主 take 接成一條,先別修。看一遍。
  3. 每段選一個主 take — 看表達,不看臉。
  4. 把 B 機素材排在 A 機素材的下層(V2),需要 jump-cut 時就切過去。
  5. 收緊到 5:00 粗剪,不擔心超時。
  6. 鎖死 S1 → S6 主軸,然後狠剪到畫面定剪 4:45。
  7. 空鏡只放有功能的:藥局綠十字、表格、走拍。不要塞填料。
  8. S5 加下三分之一字卡(Keynote 匯出 PNG)。
  9. 調色:Apple Log → Rec. 709 LUT(iPhone 內建)+ 對比 +5 + 色溫 +200K 偏暖。
  10. 混音:-16 LUFS 整體。Voice Isolation。無配樂。
  11. 片尾卡 5 秒。總長 4:50。輸出 3840×2160 · 24fps · H.264。
