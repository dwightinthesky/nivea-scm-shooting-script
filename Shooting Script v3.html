運鏡哲學 · 為什麼兩支 iPhone 可以做到大師級

看完這頁再開拍
Working principle

大師級不是「器材好」,是「決定堅定」。
每一個鏡位、每一個運鏡、每一個剪接點都有一個非它不可的理由。

整支片我們只用 三種運鏡(Locked / Slow Push / Walk),只用 三個鏡位(機位 A / 機位 B / 走廊),只用 一種光源(WeWork 北向窗光)。重複本身就是電影語言 — 你回到同一張椅子三次,觀眾的潛意識會把它讀成「論文式結構」,這比花俏的運鏡更高級。

01 · 限制 = 紀律

不要嘗試「掩飾」器材限制。iPhone 拍出來就是 iPhone 的質感。重點是用 iPhone 的優勢(極淺景深 Cinematic mode、ProRes Log、超寬動態範圍)去做專業相機都未必能做的事。

02 · 雙機位是奢侈

大部分申請影片只有一支機。你有兩支,意思是每一個 take 都同時被兩個角度記錄。這讓你能在一次表演裡剪出 jump-cut 而不破連戲,媲美雙機位紀錄片訪談。

03 · MacBook 是隱形主角

MacBook 在拍攝現場做兩件事:① 透過連續互通相機(Continuity Camera)當外接 4K 監看螢幕,讓你能看到構圖;② 開啟備忘錄全螢幕當提詞器,字級調到從鏡頭距離剛好讀得到。

Segment 2 · Retail Execution · the department store floor

1:00
A + B 換景
逐字稿

"In 2023, when I was seventeen, I interned as a floor supervisor at Far Eastern Department Stores in Taiwan. I was responsible for two floors. Menswear and sportswear. Around forty brands in total."

"Most of the counter staff had much more experience than I did. So in the beginning, I learned to listen first."

"The moment I want to share is Mother's Day. In Taiwan, it is a very important campaign period for department stores. But there was a problem."

"The main shoppers were women and families. And my floors were menswear and sportswear. So we had to ask a simple question. How do we bring the right shoppers to a floor they would normally skip?"

"We noticed that the cinema upstairs brought in a lot of foot traffic. So we used that as a starting point. We proposed a free in-store yoga series during the campaign. Three times a week. The idea was not just to offer a discount. It was to create a real reason for women and families to enter the space."

"During the campaign, store visits went up by nearly twenty percent. Mother's Day revenue grew fifteen percent year on year. And visitor-to-buyer conversion improved by eight points."

"What I learned is that retail is not only about the product on the shelf. Sometimes, it is about who is not walking past it yet."

"That is why S&CM interests me. It connects the shopper, the channel, the display, and the commercial result."

拍攝位置②

Segment 4 · Cross-Cultural Lens · Taipei and Paris

1:00
B 手持走拍
逐字稿

"I transferred to ESCP in Paris by choice. Not only because I wanted to study abroad. But because I wanted to understand a market I did not already know."

"One thing I noticed very quickly is that retail in Taiwan and France works differently. In Taiwan, skincare often sits in a very promotional environment. You see discounts. KOL recommendations. Functional claims. And clear labels telling you what the product does."

"In France, especially in pharmacies, skincare feels closer to dermatology. The feeling is more about trust. Professional advice. And products that look clinically reliable."

"For me, this is very interesting. It can be the same brand. Even the same NIVEA blue tin. But the reason people trust it can change depending on the market."

"My French is still improving. But that also makes me observe stores differently. When you are not fully native in a market, you notice where a shopper may get confused. You notice the signage. The claims. The packaging. And the shelf logic."

"That perspective matters to me. Because S&CM is not only about what the brand wants to say. It is also about what the shopper actually understands."

現場流程 · 拍攝順序

約 3.5 小時

拍攝順序

  1. 09:30 到 WeWork。確認預訂空間(熱桌 + 玻璃會議室)。看窗戶日照方向。
  2. 09:45 換裝 + 兩支 iPhone 都接外接電源。MacBook 開 Continuity Camera 對 A 機。錄 30 秒環境音。
  3. 10:00 架設機位 A(熱桌)。A 機(三腳架,1.4m)+ B 機(45°,2×)+ MacBook 提詞器。對焦 + 白平衡 + 音量。
  4. 10:20 連拍 S1 / S5 / S6 — 同一機位、同一光、各 3 take。S5 最長,留多一點時間。不要中途換景。
  5. 11:15 移到玻璃會議室。重新架 A 機(0.9m,2×)+ B 機(廣角空間)。
  6. 11:30 拍 S3(整段一鏡)+ S2 母親節特寫補拍。S3 是長 take,提詞要分四頁。
  7. 12:00 回機位 A 拍 S2 寬景。
  8. 12:15 走廊拍 S4 走拍。B 機手持,至少 3 趟。需要有人在後方護航。
  9. 12:30 B 機俯拍 b-roll:MacBook 螢幕上 mock 表格。
  10. 12:45 出 WeWork,Rue de Madrid 街上拍藥局綠十字 × 2 個交叉口。
  11. 13:00 收工。離開前用 AirDrop 把所有 take 傳 MacBook 備份。

iPhone 拍攝設定 · 必做清單

  • □ Blackmagic Camera(免費)取代內建相機:能鎖白平衡、ISO、快門。
  • □ 影片格式:ProRes 4K 24fps Log(iPhone 15 Pro 以上)。
  • □ 兩支 iPhone 設定完全相同:同 ISO、同快門 1/48、同白平衡 5200K。
  • □ 對焦鎖定主角眼睛(長按螢幕直到出現 AE/AF LOCK)。
  • □ 飛航模式 ON(避免來電/通知中斷錄製)。
  • □ 兩支同時開錄 + 拍手對時(後製用)。
  • □ 每段拍完 3 take 就 AirDrop 到 MacBook 備份,避免空間爆滿。
  • □ MacBook 螢幕亮度調最低,避免反光進 iPhone 鏡頭。

燈光 · 用 WeWork 的窗光當主光

  • 主光: 北向窗 45° 在攝影機右側。陽光太強時用百葉柔化或退到牆邊。
  • 補光: 用 MacBook 開純白螢幕當銀板補光,放主角左前 1.2m。意外有效。
  • 背景: 讓 WeWork 內部柔散成 bokeh — 畫面內不要出現品牌 logo,不要拍到其他人。
  • 玻璃室: 關走廊側百葉,控制主角後方玻璃反光。
  • 白平衡: 5200K 鎖死。絕不要用自動。
  • 時段: 中午前拍完 — 巴黎五月的光在 10:00–12:00 之間最柔。

音訊 · 自拍片最常死在這裡

  • 主聲源: iPhone #2 用 Voice Memo 放在主角面前桌上(鏡頭外)。比相機麥近 3 倍。
  • 備份: A 機本身的麥克風。對時用第一聲拍手。
  • 關空調 如果 WeWork 允許。不行的話,離出風口 1 米以上。
  • 茶水間咖啡機 會毀掉一整個 take。每次開錄前先聽。
  • 每個機位 30 秒環境音 — 開拍前先錄,不要後錄。
  • 音量峰值: ‑12dB,底噪 ‑40dB。全程戴耳機。
  • 後製: Final Cut 內建 Voice Isolation 效果可以救 90% 的問題。

剪輯順序

  2. 組合剪:所有主 take 接成一條,先別修。看一遍。
  3. 每段選一個主 take — 看表達,不看臉。
  4. 把 B 機素材排在 A 機素材的下層(V2),需要 jump-cut 時就切過去。
